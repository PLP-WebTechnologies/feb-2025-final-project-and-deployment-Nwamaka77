This suya spice is made with a perfect blend of traditional ingredients to give you the authentic taste of Nigerian street food. Enjoy the deep flavor that has been perfected over generations.

Our Premium Suya Spice blend contains carefully selected peanuts, ginger, garlic, paprika, and other authentic spices to create the perfect balance of flavors.

Weight: 100g

Category: Meat Seasonings

Tags: Suya, Grilling

Product Details

Our Premium Suya Spice is carefully blended to bring the authentic taste of Nigerian street barbecue to your home. Perfect for grilling meat, poultry, and even vegetables.

  • 100% natural ingredients
  • No preservatives or artificial additives
  • Perfect for barbecues and grilling
  • Shelf life: 12 months from production date
  • Made in Nigeria

Ingredients

Ground peanuts, ginger, garlic, paprika, cayenne pepper, onion powder, salt, and other natural spices.

Allergen Information: Contains peanuts. Produced in a facility that also processes other nuts and gluten.

How to Use

For the perfect Suya experience:

  1. Thinly slice your choice of meat (beef, chicken, or lamb works best)
  2. Coat the meat with a little oil
  3. Generously sprinkle the suya spice on all sides
  4. Let marinate for at least 30 minutes (2-4 hours for best results)
  5. Grill on high heat until cooked to your preference
  6. Serve with sliced onions and tomatoes
